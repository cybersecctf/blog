Noxtal's Cyberworld

Welcome! I'm Noxtal and this is my "Cyberworld". In other words, it is a website on which I post writeups for cybersecurity challenges, tutorials and offer good ways to learn programming and hacking. My goal is to make people from the coding and hacking community learn and share the things I create. I am not in any way a certified hacker or pentester, but I do that as a passion because of my young age. Enjoy your stay!
As you have probably noticed, it's been a long time since I've last uploaded. That's because I'm currently taking my yearly pause of computers and all... I find it really important to distance myself from this passion every autumn, else I easily start to burn out. I'm also working hard on music at the moment, which is another of my passions.
Hopefully, I should be back soon, stay tuned!
